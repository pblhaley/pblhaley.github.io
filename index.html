A Day in the Life of a Competitive Rower

Do you have what it takes to become a great competitive rower? Let's take a look at a typical day for some of our most competitive rowers today!

Daily Schedule:

  • 4:30am: Time to wake up!
  • 5:00am: Meet at the boat dock for a warm-up.
  • 5:30am: Hit the water for your first workout of the day.
  • 7:00am: Finish up your workout and grab some breakfast.
  • 8:00am: Head to your daily job.
  • 12:00pm: Lunch!
  • 3:00pm: Time for your second workout of the day, weightlifting!
  • 5:00pm: Finish up your reps and grab some dinner.
  • 7:00pm: If you are having a good day, squeeze in another hour on the water in the evening.
  • 10:00pm: Hit the hay and get ready to wake up tomorrow to do it all over again!
